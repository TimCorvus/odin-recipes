Cold Italian Pasta Salad

Description

Italian pasta salad is perfect year-round. No matter the occassion, everyone loves a fresh pasta salad loaded with different flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella
  • Cooked pasta
  • Salami
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Peper
  • Oil
  • Balsamic

Steps

  1. Combine cucumber, tomato, fresh mozzarella, cooked pasta and salami in a large bowl
  2. Add in basil and season to taste with salt and pepper.
  3. Toss in oil and balsamic to taste. Serve!
